Privacy Policy

We at whispertranscribe.com value the privacy rights of our users and acknowledge the significance of safeguarding the personal information we gather from you. Our privacy policy is intended to provide you with clarity on the information we collect, its usage and sharing.

This includes the whispertranscribe.com (The “Site”), the App you can download, and all associated tools and services (collectively referred to as the “Whispertranscribe Service”). We may modify this Privacy Policy periodically. Should any changes occur, we will notify you by updating the date at the top of the policy and, in certain cases, we may provide additional notice (such as displaying an announcement on our homepage or sending you an email notification). We encourage you to review the Privacy Policy whenever you access the WhisperTranscribe Service to remain informed about our information practices and the methods you can use to help protect your privacy. If you use or access our website and services, you agree to comply with the practices outlined in this privacy policy.

Personal Information We Collect

Website Visitors

If you visit whispertranscribe.com and interact with the website, such as by contacting us or requesting support, you may share personal information. We will only collect personal data that is reasonable or necessary for the purpose of your contact with us. Any personal information you share with us will only be used for the stated purposes in our privacy policy.

Active Users and Customers

In order to utilize our services, it may be necessary for you to provide personally identifiable information, including but not limited to your full name and email address. When making a purchase on our website, our payment processor LemonSqueezy will collect and process your complete credit card information directly through their secure Checkout service. We do not retain nor store your credit card details. From time to time, we may use your personal information, such as your email address, to contact you with information that may be of interest, usually pertaining to product updates. The option to unsubscribe from such messages can be exercised by clicking the designated "unsubscribe" link located at the bottom of our emails.

Data

While using our services, you may be able to create or upload data in text or audio format. Some of this information may be stored and maintained on our servers provided by OVH. You may, for example, upload spoken audio files to our website and use our services to generate written content from those audio files. In general, audio files are removed from our OVH-hosted servers once they have been successfully transcribed. The generated written content is saved on the device that you installed the WhisperTranscribe app on.

Usage and Analytics Information

We use common tools like cookies and log files to collect information from visitors of whispertranscribe.com.

Essential Cookies are small files that help us remember your login information and other details about a user session.

Non-essential cookies while not essential for the fundamental functionality of the website, serve multiple purposes. At whispertranscribe, our utilization of non-essential cookies encompasses the following objectives:

  • Track affiliate purchases, allowing us to attribute sales to specific affiliates accurately.
  • Monitor conversions at advertising platforms, aiding us in assessing the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.
  • Gather user analytics, providing valuable insights into user behavior and interactions with our platform.

Log files are used to collect information about your visit, such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. We use this information to analyze trends and conduct user research.

Information usage, in summary

Personal Contact Information is utilized for essential purposes including account administration, service delivery, user identification/authentication, and addressing issues such as unauthorized utilization of services. Additionally, this information may be employed for targeted marketing initiatives, which typically involve email communications providing product updates and relevant information. It's important to note that users retain the right to opt out of receiving these communications. The option to unsubscribe from such messages can be exercised by clicking the designated "unsubscribe" link located at the bottom of our emails.

Billing Information is used for managing accounts, providing services, and collecting payments.

Usage and Analytics Information is used for analyzing trends, improving user experience, tracking and aggregating non-personal information, and providing services.
